Starlink Mini brings space internet to backpackers

The Wi-Fi router is baked into the laptop-sized dish which can be powered by a standard USB power bank.

The new compact DC-powered Starlink Mini is about the size of a thick laptop and integrates the Wi-Fi router right inside the dish. And despite using less power than other Starlink terminals, it can still deliver speeds over 100Mbps. "This product will change the world," claimed SpaceX CEO Elon Musk on X, saying it took less than five minutes to setup.
